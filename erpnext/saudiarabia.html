ERPNext isn’t just software it’s a platform to help Saudi businesses run better, faster, and smarter. Built with the Kingdom’s needs in mind, it brings everything together – finance, sales, inventory, HR, projects, manufacturing, and more – into one simple, connected system. BENEFITS

Make your business ready for the future

One app to manage the entire business

ERPNext is feature rich. From Manufacturing to Services and SMBs to Large Enterprises, it works for everyone. It also integrates with many other apps built by Frappe like CRM, HR & Payroll, etc.

Explore Frappe apps

No per-user pricing; pay only for hosting

Businesses typically spend 2-6% of their revenue on proprietary SaaS, which keeps growing exponentially with time thanks to per-user pricing plans. However, in case of hosting costs, you pay only for the cloud services utilized, which do not linearly increase with scale.

Explore ERPNext pricing

Customizable; No code, Low code

Design forms, reports, print formats, and dashboards as per your requirement and automate tasks with drag-and-drop simplicity. All this is possible because ERPNext is built on Frappe’s very own Frappe Framework.

Explore Frappe Framework

Enterprise grade security & privacy

ERPNext is ISO 27001 and GDPR compliant. Being powered by AWS gives it extended security benefits like DDoS protection. It is also equipped with 2FA, IP restriction, and field-level roles & permissions, which provide an added layer of security.

Explore Frappe's security stack

No vendor lockin

ERPNext comes with a default hosting option on Frappe Cloud. But you can host it on your cloud as well. You can choose to implement it yourself or work with one of our certified partners. All this, without any compulsions of working with the same setup in future.

Explore Frappe Cloud
