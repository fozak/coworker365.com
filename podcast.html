The Frappe Podcast

Frappe has more than just great products, it has great stories too.

Our community is full of people who are driven by intention. Their stories speak of winding journeys with unexpected obstacles and the determination to take the less-travelled path. Each, a reminder that there is more than one way to build something meaningful.

Read more

The Frappe Podcast began with a simple thought. What if we could make a home for these stories? Not just the milestones, but the moments that reveal who someone really is. The hard choices and the values they refuse to sacrifice. The small turning points that often go unnoticed.

Each episode is a conversation guided by curiosity. Sometimes we talk about work, but more often we wander into questions that dont fit on a resume. The ones that stay on our minds long after the mic is off.

If even one of these stories sparks someones belief in doing things differently, then this podcast will be worth it.

- Foram Shah, Host
