Frequently asked questions

Are there any limits on the number of tickets, agents, or customers based on pricing?
No, Frappe Helpdesk offers unlimited tickets, agents, and customers on all plans. We dont believe in restricting growth. Whether youre a small support team or a large enterprise, you get full access without per-agent pricing.
How can I get consultation?
You can connect with one of our official partners for consultation and implementation support.
What is Priority SLA?
Priority SLA includes faster response and resolution time as mentioned in Table II of our Service Level Agreement (SLA)
Do you have discounts for education institutes and non profits?
Yes, we support education institutes and non profits. Contact us to apply the discount.
What kind of support is included in the plans?
All Frappe Helpdesk plans include hosting-related support on Frappe Cloud. If youre on the $50/month plan or higher, you also get product support (bug fixes, guidance, and feature-related help) under our product warranty.
Is there a free plan available?
Yes! You can self-host Frappe Helpdesk for free since it's fully open-source. If you prefer a managed solution, you can host it on Frappe Cloud, starting at just $5/month.
I have more questions.
Drop us a message here and our team will get in touch with you!
