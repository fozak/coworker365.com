Venue and stay

In order to make your journey and experience during the conference better, here are some details that will help you with the stay and travel.

Reaching the venue

By Air

If youre flying in, the nearest airport is Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (BOM). You can take a prepaid taxi or book an Uber/Ola from the airport. Depending on traffic, it may take 45 minutes to 1 hour to reach the venue.

By Local Transport

Take a local train to Elphinstone Road (now called Prabhadevi) or Lower Parel station on the Western Line. From there, it is a 1015 minute cab ride to Nehru Centre, Worli. Alternatively, you can take a BEST bus to the Worli Naka or Nehru Planetarium stop, both within walking distance from the venue.

By Private Transport

You can book a cab via Uber or Ola app for a direct ride to Nehru Centre Auditorium. If youre driving, use Google Maps to navigate to Nehru Centre, Dr Annie Besant Road, Worli, Mumbai. On-site parking is available.
