Product Keynote

Every year, the Frappe team along with the community showcases what they have been working on for the past year and plan to release in the near future.

With more products than before, the product owners of each of these products come and share their experiences working with users and trying to come up with new solutions to solve their problems.

Community gathering

Frappeverse is where the builders and the dreamers of the Frappe ecosystem come together. It's a space where product keynotes set a direction, community stories inspire and open discussions bring ideas to life - all while celebrating the spirit of open-source.

Community stories

It is always exciting to hear how an ever expanding global community of users is using Coworker365 Products and Integrations to meet business requirements.

Expect to hear a wide range of stories from technology oriented entrepreneurs to freelancers to folks who work with non profits and governments. In the past, people have spoken about how the National Dairy board has implemented ERPNext in more than 30 co-operatives, to how the government of Saudi Arabia is planning to deploy ERPNext in their health sector.
