Welcome to the ERPNext Stock forum. The Stock module has features for managing warehouses, Items, Price Lists, Item Variants, Material Requests, Purchase Receipts, Stock Entry, Landed Cost Voucher, Delivery Trip, Stock Reconciliation.

In this category, you can post questions, discuss, and help other community members with Inventory related questions that pertain to ERPNext.

Before asking a question here, do go through the official ERPNext documentation to see if you can find your answers to your questions.

Here are some common topics in Stock/Inventory that users may need help with:

  1. Warehouse
  2. Item
  3. Opening Stock
  4. Price Lists
  5. Item Variants
  6. Item Price
  7. Material Request
  8. Purchase Receipt
  9. Delivery Note
  10. Stock Entry
  11. Landed Cost Voucher
  12. Stock Reconciliation
