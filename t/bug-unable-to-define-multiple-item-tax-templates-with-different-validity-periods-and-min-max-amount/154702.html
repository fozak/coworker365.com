Bug: Unable to Define Multiple Item Tax Templates with Different Validity Periods and Min/Max Amount

ERPNext India Compliance
loginid24x7 September 27, 2025, 7:42pm 1

While configuring Item Tax Templates for GST compliance, I encountered a serious limitation in the Item Tax Template Valid From / Min-Max Amount feature.

The system does not allow defining the same Item Tax Template more than once , even if:

  • The Valid From dates are different, and
  • The Min Amount / Max Amount ranges are different.

This becomes a problem in real-world tax scenarios where the tax structure changes over time but for the same item category and tax template.

Real Example: Footwear GST Slab Change (India)

Here’s a real GST compliance scenario that demonstrates the problem:

  • Before 22 Sept 2025:
    • GST on footwear above ₹1000 → 18%
  • From 22 Sept 2025 onwards:
    • GST on footwear above ₹2500 → 18%

In this case, we must define two valid tax conditions for the same item tax template :

  1. One for invoices before 22 Sep 2025 (Valid From: e.g., 01 Jan 2025 – 21 Sep 2025)
  2. Another for invoices on or after 22 Sep 2025 (Valid From: 22 Sep 2025 onwards)

However, ERPNext blocks this configuration because it does not allow creating the same Item Tax Template again, even if the date range and min/max conditions differ.
