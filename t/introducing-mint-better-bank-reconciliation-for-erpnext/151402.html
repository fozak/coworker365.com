It‚Äôs an open source Frappe app so you can install it on your server. Not restricted to just Frappe Cloud.

For credit cards: the account is a liability account (root type is Liability (not asset) - so it‚Äôs on the balance sheet), but the ‚Äúaccount type‚Äù is set to ‚ÄúBank‚Äù. This allows you to pick the account when creating a ‚ÄúBank Account‚Äù - the bank account should be a company bank account.

So if you had an expense on the credit card, you would credit the account (your liability increases) and debit the expense account. If you made a payment, you would debit the account (your liability decreases).

The ‚Äúis_credit_card‚Äù checkbox is only for Mint to know which type of Journal Entry to create - ‚ÄúCredit Card Entry‚Äù vs ‚ÄúBank Entry‚Äù - it has no impact on accounting - it just looks better when viewing the general ledger.
