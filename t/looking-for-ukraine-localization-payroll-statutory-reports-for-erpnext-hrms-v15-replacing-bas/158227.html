  3. Pay schemes: monthly salary / hourly pay / bonuses / allowances.

    • Average headcount (average listed number of employees)

    • Staff turnover / personnel movement

    • SSC/ESV reporting

    • 1DF (personal income reporting)

    • Military tax

    • Employee turnover rate

    • Personnel structure

    • Worked time

    • Leaves/vacations

    • Payroll fund (FOP)

    • Average salary

    • Total personnel cost

    • Breakdown by responsibility centers (cost centers)

    • Automatic generation of electronic payslips.

  6. Integrations: M.E.Doc, Ukrainian banks, e-document exchange / e-signature.
