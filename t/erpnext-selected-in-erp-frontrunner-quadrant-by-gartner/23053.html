ERPNext Selected in ERP FrontRunner Quadrant by Gartner

Announcements
rmehta April 26, 2017, 7:06am 1

Community,

Excited to announce that ERPNext was included in the ERP FrontRunners List by Gartner (SoftwareAdvice.com). This is one of the best external endorsements for ERPNext yet, and it was nice to see ERPnext listed alongside top players like SAP, Microsoft and SalesForce! ERPNext was listed as a winner, ahead of 588 other ERP products that were listed as runners up!
