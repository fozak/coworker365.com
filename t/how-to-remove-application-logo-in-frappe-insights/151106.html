Hi @arya_criton,

Insights doesn’t have a feature to change the logo from settings. This is requested by many users (see GitHub issues #652, #642, #326) and may come in future versions.

Manual Workaround (Insights v2)

File: apps/insights/frontend/src/components/Sidebar.vue

Lines 7 and 10:

  1. Add your logo to apps/insights/frontend/src/assets/
  2. Update lines 7 and 10 with your logo filename
  3. Rebuild: bench build --app insights

Hope this helps!
