Hello,

GSTR-9 is not supported out of the box in ERPNext India Compliance.

You can use helper reports like GST Purchase Register Beta, GST Sales Register Beta, GST Account-wise Summary, GST Tax Rate-wise Summary, etc., to consolidate GSTR-9 data.
