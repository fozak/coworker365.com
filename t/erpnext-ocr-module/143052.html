#ERPNext_OCR_Module
So, what if you could scan any document and have the data instantly extracted into ERPNext?
Manually reading and entering text from invoices, receipts, and documents is slow and painful. So I made an AI-powered OCR module that integrates seamlessly with ERPNext to automate text extraction.

What does this module do?

** Upload any scanned document or image (Invoices, Receipts, Contracts, etc.)
** AI reads and extracts text with high accuracy
** Automatically fills ERPNext fields based on extracted data
** Supports multi-document processing at once
** Works inside ERPNext with a simple upload & process button
