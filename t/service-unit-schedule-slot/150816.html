akurungadam August 3, 2025, 5:58am 5

The system currently allows scheduling, based on times lots, for Practitioner only.

However, appointments can be booked against Service Unit / Department (without Practitioner) based on the configuration in Appointment Type. No schedule slots can be created, you are allowed to book appointments without any validation.

Its worth exploring, please see if it fits your use case.
