Deprecating ERPNext mobile apps

Announcements
ChillarAnand December 28, 2021, 10:32am 1

ERPNext mobile apps are available on Android & iOS app stores. Currently, these apps just have basic functionality as mentioned here. Adding full support for these apps takes lot of development & maintenance work.

Frappe/ERPNext web views are mobile friendly, has full support for ERPNext features, need less maintenance and users can directly access sites from the mobile browser itself.

Due to this, we planned to deprecate mobile apps and we will be delisting the apps on both the stores.

If anyone from the community is interested in taking the mobile app development forward, let us know.

If you are using ERPNext mobile apps, consider using mobile browser to access ERPNext.
