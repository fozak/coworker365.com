Hello Shibam,

I have built a module called “Hospitality Core” specifically for ERPNext.

It covers 90% of your list out-of-the-box:

  1. Front Desk: Check-in/out, Automatic Night Audit, and Room Moves.

  2. POS Bridge: I have written a custom bridge that takes ERPNext POS invoices and posts them directly to the Guest Folio automatically.

  3. Housekeeping & Maintenance: Fully integrated with the room status logic.

  4. Accounting: Automatic “City Ledger” handling for corporate clients (auto-transferring guest debts to company accounts).

For Channel Management, since every hotel uses different providers (SiteMinder, RateGain, etc.), I handle this via customization. Since I am the developer of the source code, I can integrate your specific OTA provider during deployment.

I would be happy to give you a demo of the current system and discuss the customization scope.
