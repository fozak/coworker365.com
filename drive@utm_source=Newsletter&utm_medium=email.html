Imagine making smarter data-driven decisions at a fraction of the cost Insights is an easy-to-use, open-source business intelligence tool designed to help businesses make the most of their data. It enables you to connect data across multiple sources, analyze them, and derive valuable insights to make informed decisions for faster growth and increased operational efficiency.

Frappe Drive is an open-source cloud storage platform. With team management, powerful S3 integration, and a document editor, it's the perfect drive solution for ERPNext users.

Collaborate powerfully

No more compressed files over chats.Drive's versatile permission system allows you to tweak access to suit your needs. Share with individuals or with a team of your choice - or make it public for the world to see.

Built for teams

Working in a company, or using Drive with family? No need to repeatedly share individual files. With a few clicks, you can create an isolated workspaces for a specific group of people, who will now have a common view of the folders and files inside the team.

Work effortlessly with as many teams as you want. You can manage and invite users in the teams you own, and join others' teams. With manager, user, and guest roles, you can perfectly manage access

Write with flow

Text editing in Drive is powered by Frappe Writer.Be it random notes, official documents, or journaling, our document editor is smooth and efficient. With realtime collaboration, automated and manual versioning, and so much more, Writer is the text editor you've been looking for.

S3 integration. Minutes, not days.

Want cheap storage on AWS, while having an amazing tool to manage your files from the web? Frappe Drive is the answer.

Add your S3 details to Drive. If you have existing files, you can use the Sync feature to immediately link the files from S3 into Drive. Get moving in minutes.

Built for Frappe

If you already use ERPNext or a Frappe product, Drive is meant for you. With tight integrations, you can add files directly from Drive into ERPNext, or store Frappe Slides presentations in Drive folders.

Coming soon: Drive Desktop

Sync your remote folders with your computer (and after this, mobile). Use Drive to sync your files between devices.

Our story

The file management software arena has stagnated. There is little innovation anymore, existing solutions are hard to use, and big tech controls the industry with impunity. Frappe Drive is our effort to combine functionality, security and great UI.

Our goal is to keep shipping new features that make file management more intuitive. These features could be small (like our smart sort) or powerful (our S3 integration), but they will save you time and effort.

At Frappe, we are not satisfied with shabby design. Like all our products, Drive is built on frappe-ui, offering a consistent and aesthetic user interface.

And we do all this while being committed to being 100% open-source; check out Drive on GitHub.

For the occasional update, or to take part in beta testing, join ourTelegram group.
