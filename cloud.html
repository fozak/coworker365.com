What is Frappe Cloud?

Application + Infrastructure

Hosting web applications with high reliability on your own can be time consuming, hard and exhausting. That's why we built Frappe Cloud, a simple, user-friendly and sophisticated open source platform to host Frappe applications with peace of mind.

It takes care of installation, setup, upgrades, monitoring, maintenance and support for your Frappe deployments. It is a fully featured developer platform with an ability to manage and control multiple Frappe deployments.

There's more! You dont pay per user, you only pay for the resources you consume.

Compute based pricing

Are you paying per user pricing for your business software?

We believe cost of software ownership should not grow with your team size. With Frappe Cloud's compute-based pricing, you can unlock the power of open source and align costs with actual usage, helping you save costs and increase scalability.

Frappe Cloud offers multiple hosting options:

Shared

Shared hosting is a cost-effective solution where multiple sites share the same server resources with a dedicated database, making it ideal for small to medium-sized projects that require basic performance and scalability. You can also opt for private benches to host multiple sites in an isolated environment.

Dedicated

Dedicated hosting offers exclusive server resources for a single client, ensuring maximum performance, security, and customisation options, suitable for high-traffic websites and resource-intensive applications. You can create multiple benches and sites under dedicated servers.
