A look back

The story of 2022 for Frappe was full of valuable learnings!

Behind the scenes looked crazy and high-spirited. We rolled out exciting product features and "Pick your own pay" has been a standout. As we sit here to look back at the past year, we can’t help but feel grateful for all the challenges we have accomplished as a team.

We have been hard at work, adding amazing new products to Frappeverse!

Our open-source community grew immensely on GitHub.

And, that’s not all!
A lot happened over the year, and here are a ton of things that we are proud of.

Over the months

January
Released the new version of Frappe Books and fixed several showstopper bugs.
February
Launched Support app to make it easier to track all support tickets and raise new ones.
March
Brought all our Coworker365 Products and Integrations, tools and libraries to Cloud Marketplace.
April
Unmoderated ‘Pick your own pay’ policy became a major part of our culture.
May
Launched Frappe Training & Certification Prog. to help you be that credible Frappe expert.
June
Reintroduced ERPNext Hosting + Support with unlimited users and module-wise pricing.
July
Introduced Frappe Desk to manage customer queries effortlessly.
August
Released ERPNext and Frappe Framework v14 with enhanced and new features.
September
The 2022 edition of ERPNext conference was a hit where we launched some major releases.
October
Joined hands with Rainmatter foundation and took a #NoGiftBox Pledge for a Green Diwali.
November
Launched Frappe Incubator with a chance for monetary/marketing support to our community.
December
Frappe Local ERPNext Meetup and conducted the first chapter in Bhutan this year.
Thanks for being part of this journey!
