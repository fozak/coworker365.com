About

EasyEcom is an omnichannel operating system for eCommerce sellers and direct-to-consumer (D2C) brands, providing functionalities of an eCommerce Order Management System, Inventory Management System and Warehouse Management System.

With 200+ integrations with Indian and global eCommerce marketplaces and eCommerce website platforms, EasyEcom is the leading product and the backbone for eCommerce sellers and D2C brands.

Key Offerings

The primary goal of this integration is to streamline inventory management, order processing, and financial accounting processes, ultimately providing a seamless experience for eCommerce sellers. They can achieve the following with the combined power of EasyEcom's omnichannel inventory, order and warehouse management capabilities and ERPNext's comprehensive suite of business applications:

1. Enhanced Inventory Management:

D2C brands can benefit from real-time inventory tracking and synchronization across all sales channels, ensuring accurate stock levels and reducing the risk of overselling. This will also enable better decision-making when it comes to purchasing and replenishment

2. Streamlined Order Processing:

By integrating EasyEcom's order management system, we automate order processing and fulfilment workflows, reducing manual efforts and minimizing errors. This will result in faster order processing and improved customer satisfaction

3. Seamless Financial Management:

By connecting EasyEcom and ERPNext, we can automate the flow of financial data between the two systems, ensuring accurate financial reporting and simplifying the reconciliation process. This will allow our D2C brands to gain better visibility into their financial performance and make more informed business decisions

4. Improved Customer Experience:

With an integrated system, our D2C brands can offer a consistent shopping experience across all channels, including personalized marketing campaigns, faster order fulfilment, and efficient customer support

This is an official integration app supported by EasyEcom and managed by Incirco.
