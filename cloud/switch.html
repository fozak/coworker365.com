Custom app management

Deploying and managing custom apps can be a hassle, from syncing updates to sharing with others. Frappe Cloud makes it easyinstall apps directly from GitHub, keep them updated automatically by deploying private benches and sites, and even list your custom apps on our marketplace for public access.

Server management

Scaling with multiple servers is often complex and resource-intensive. Frappe Cloud provides dedicated resources like compute, storage, and network capacity so you can launch and manage new servers easily. With Private Benches, SSH Access, and Database Access, scaling is smooth and efficient, letting you focus on what matters.

Backup and recovery

Data loss can set you back, especially if backups arent consistent. Frappe Cloud automates backups every six hours and stores 1 in 4 offsite for extra security. You can also trigger backups manually anytime.

Analytics

Tracking performance across scattered data sources is inconvenient. Frappe Cloud puts all your key metrics in one placefrom uptime and database logs to CPU usage and background jobs. Get clear insights through simple charts and make smarter decisions to keep your site running smoothly.

Automatic updates

Manual updates consume valuable time, especially with security patches and new features. On Frappe Cloud, updates are automated or scheduled according to your preferences, so your apps stay current without any extra work from you.

Multi-region hosting

Slow load times frustrate users, especially when hosting isnt nearby. With more than 10 global server locations, Frappe Cloud lets you choose the region closest to your audience for lower latency, faster response time, and smoother performance.
